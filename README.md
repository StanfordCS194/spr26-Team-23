# Tunnel
 
As people increasingly move to AI chatbots, companies need ways to track and improve how their products and services are mentioned and discussed by these tools. We're building **Tunnel**, a platform that gives companies visibility into AI-generated brand mentions and actionable insights to improve how they appear in AI responses.
 
Read more on our [wiki](https://github.com/StanfordCS194/spr26-Team-23/wiki).
